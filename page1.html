Mobile Legends Characters

Name Picture Info
Fanny
Fanny is a fast and agile assassin hero in Mobile Legends known for her ability to quickly zip around the map using her Steel Cable skill, which allows her to latch onto walls and move at high speeds. Her high mobility, combined with her burst damage, makes her a deadly threat in the hands of skilled players, able to eliminate squishy enemies in seconds. Fanny's ultimate, Cut Throat, deals massive damage, making her a potent force in team fights when used strategically.
Diggie
Diggie is a support hero in Mobile Legends known for his ability to provide crowd control and protect allies with his Time Journey ultimate, which grants immunity to disables for a brief period. He can also use Nesting Owl to provide vision and deal magic damage over time, while his Compact Star can stun enemies and provide additional crowd control. With his unique skillset, Diggie excels at disrupting enemy teams and keeping his teammates safe, making him a valuable asset in team compositions.
Harith
Harith is a fast and agile assassin in Mobile Legends, known for his high mobility and burst damage. His Zaman Force allows him to dash around the battlefield, dealing damage and gaining movement speed, while his Chrono Dash grants him incredible repositioning and evasion capabilities. With his Temporal Turbulence ultimate, Harith can slow enemies and deal massive damage, making him a formidable threat in team fights and skirmishes.
