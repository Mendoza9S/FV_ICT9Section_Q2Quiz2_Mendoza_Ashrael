Name: Bitcoin
Definition: Bitcoin operates on peer-to-peer technology, eliminating the need for banks. Transactions and the creation of new bitcoins are managed by a central network. As an open-source platform, Bitcoin's design is publicly available, meaning no one person or entity owns or controls it. Through many of its unique properties, Bitcoin allows exciting uses that could not be covered by any previous payment system.
